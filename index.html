About us

Welcome to ATHWAX Art Gallery, where creativity meets craftsmanship. At ATHWAX, we specialize in transforming spaces with our stunning mosaic art, captivating paintings, intricate collage artwork, and elegant wall hangings. Our gallery is a haven for art lovers and collectors, showcasing a diverse range of styles and techniques that reflect the rich cultural heritage and contemporary aesthetics. Each piece is meticulously crafted by my gifted hands, which i use to bring passion, innovation, and a unique perspective to my work. At ATHWAX Art Gallery, i am dedicated to enriching lives through art, inspiring creativity, and building a community of art enthusiasts who appreciate the beauty and significance of visual expression. Come and experience the magic of art at ATHWAX, where every piece tells a story.

What we do

  • At ATHWAX Art Gallery, my process begins with a deep understanding of the visual arts. We explore various themes and subjects, drawing inspiration from the world around us, from the smallest details in nature to the grandest architectural structures.
  • Each artwork starts as a concept, meticulously sketched and planned to ensure every detail aligns with my vision. For my mosaic pieces, we carefully select each tile, considering color, texture, and material to create a harmonious and visually striking composition.
  • As I move from the planning stage to execution, precision and attention to detail become paramount. I painstakingly piece together each mosaic, paint each stroke, and assemble each collage element with dedication and passion.
  • Finally, I ensure that every artwork is ready for display. I carefully inspects each piece, making any necessary adjustments to ensure it meets high standards of quality. Once complete, my artworks are ready to transform spaces and inspire those who encounter them.
